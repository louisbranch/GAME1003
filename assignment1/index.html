About

Memory Game is challenging and has been a favorite for generations! The game itself requires concentration, observation and the ability to memorize and match pairs.

This game was created as a final project for the Assets Development I class for George Brown College - Game Programming program.

Rules

The objective of the game is to collect the most matching pairs in a short period of time. The faster you match them, the higher you score will be.

There are three levels of difficult:

  1. Easy - 12 pairs
  2. Medium - 15 pairs
  3. Hard - 18 pairs

Once all the cards have been played, you will be able to find your score.
